USS Yankee
   The USS Yankee was a 406 ft. 1½in. long, Iron hulled, Auxiliary Cruiser of Spanish American War vintage. December 5, 1908, heavy seas caused the steamer to founder in Buzzard's Bay, while in tow to New Bedford.
